DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE & ENGINEERING

VISION

To escalate the department by creativeness in technology for emerging and global needs through Training, Research & Development.

MISSION

To impart training to students in emerging technologies to product development

  1. To promote research & innovation skills through professional guidance
  2. To originate social technocrats who perceive societal problems as being solvable by applying technology.
  3. To produce professionals with strong values and zeal to serve society.
  4. To create leaders with strong communication skills who can drive, inspire, and guide the groups they work with.
